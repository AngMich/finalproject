About EpicPanel Comics

Sharing our love for comics, one panel at a time.

Our Mission

Welcome to EpicPanel Comics — a place built by fans, for fans. We’re all about helping you explore your favorite stories and discover new ones.

Whether you're into superheroes, indie titles, manga or graphic novels, EpicPanel offers a clean and simple way to browse, review, and collect your favorite comics.

Meet the Creator

Angelos Michalopoulos is the developer behind EpicPanel Comics. He created this platform as part of a personal project combining a passion for technology with a love of comic books.

The site is built using Django and Bootstrap, with an emphasis on usability, clarity, and fun.
